PhysioFriends

Our Idea

PhysioFriends uses a series of personal questions to determine the ideal workout for you while taking into account your restrictions, mood, and physical state. It just takes a simple few steps. First fill out the survey in the "Find-A-Workout" tab, and you will recive a link for your personalized work out! By using PhysioFriends you are able to have a stressfree, personalized workout without having to pay for a personal trainer!
